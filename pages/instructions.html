Instructions

Decks & Folders

  • Click the Decks page in the navigation bar above to be able to view, study, create, edit & delete decks.
  • You are also able to create folders which allow you to organise your decks more, indicated by a folder icon.
  • In the Decks page click the [+] button in the top right corner which will allow you to create a deck or folder.
  • To add cards to a newly created deck click the pencil icon to go into edit mode. You need at least 1 card in a deck to be able to study it.
  • With any deck or folder click the pencil icon to edit it and the bin icon to delete it.
  • Click the name of a folder (in blue) to go into it.
  • Click the name of a deck (in blue) to access study options (Make sure it has at least 1 card!)

Study Options

  • When clicking on a deck, you access its study options: Normal Mode and Spaced Repetition.
  • Normal Mode takes all the cards, shuffles them, and presents them to you one by one.
  • Spaced Repetition makes it so the more you continously know a card well, the less of a chance it has to show up in the future.
  • This point of this is allowing you to revise the harder cards more, and occasionally get the cards you know really well later on to ensure you still know them.

Studying a Deck

  • With whatever study mode you've selected, you will see a front of a card on your screen.
  • Once you've thought of the answer, click it to reveal its back.
  • 3 rating options will appear to ask you how well you knew the card.
  • This response is used for the spaced repetition mode.
